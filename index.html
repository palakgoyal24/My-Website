About Me

I, Palak Goyal, hail from the princely state of Rajasthan. Rajasthan happens to be one of the favourite tourist spots for foreigners, with its old-world charm of variegated colours, valor and romanticism. The dessert states capital is Jaipur, where I have stayed and studied. I have had excellent schooling from India International School, Jaipur after which I did my B.Tech in I.T. from SVKM’s NMIMS University’s MPSTME, Mumbai,India and finally MSc in Information Systems from University of Surrey, Guildford, UK. During my undergrad, I was fortunate enough to get placed at Accenture Services Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore where I have worked for a period of 14 months. After Accenture, I started working at our own organization, Pratahkal, Jaipur where I worked for 2 years and decided to pursue my Masters. After my Masters I came back to my hometown and currently working at Prathakal to help improve their website.
